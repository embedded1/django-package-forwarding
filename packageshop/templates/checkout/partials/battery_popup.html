{% blocktrans %} Due to regulations by the IATA, FAA and the TSA, USendHome no longer will be able to ship loose Lithium Ion batteries (PI965) for our customers. This includes spare cell phone, and Laptop batteries, as well as power banks, and mobile chargers. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} In order to meet the new regulations and to avoid situations where the package is returned back to us we need your cooperation with labeling your package before it is released for international delivery. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} USendHome will not compensate shipping charges if carrier rejects and returns the package back to us due to false labeling. You can order the Customized Services service in case you don't know if your package contains batteries. We will examine the content and apply the correct labeling. {% endblocktrans %}
