{% trans "How long can I store my packages in your warehouse?" %}

{% blocktrans %} We offer 10 days of free storage for a single package and 30 days for consolidated packages. We will notify you by email 3 days before your free storage quota runs out. A fee will be applied for each day the free quota is exceeded. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} The maximum storage time is 90 days. After that, the goods will be considered abandoned and we may dispose of them in any manner permitted by law. {% endblocktrans %}
