{% trans "How to get Express Checkout for my parcel?" %}

{% trans "Introduction" %}

{% blocktrans %} We process packages in First-In-First-Processed order. This includes extra services and out-for-delivery processing. Expedite your package processing by ordering this extra service and get your package pushed to the top of the list so your shopping gets delivered faster. There are 2 ways to order the Express Checkout service. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Walkthrough" %}

There are 2 ways to request the Express Checkout service for your parcel:

  • Order the Express Checkout service in advance (20% off retail service price): This method allows you to order the service in advance for all incoming packages.
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Scroll down the right part of the page until you see “Order Extra Service in Advance”. Within the option, select “Express Checkout” and click the Save button.
  • Order the Express Checkout service after we receive your package:
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Click the “Manage Packages” button.
    • This will lead you to your packages we have received. Now click on “Extras” button for your preferred package.
    • This will open a popup window from where you can select Express Checkout for your parcel and click on the Order button.
    • {% trans "We'll notify you by email once we've completed your express checkout request so you can authorize your shipment." %}
