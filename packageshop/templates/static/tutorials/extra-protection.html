{% trans "How to add extra protection for my parcel?" %}

{% trans "Introduction" %}

{% blocktrans %} Adding extra protection with each of your parcel is easy and just take few clicks. There are 2 ways to request Extra Protection with your parcels. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Walkthrough" %}

There are 2 ways to request the Extra Protection service for your parcel:

  • Order the Extra Protection service in advance (20% off retail service price): This method allows you to the service in advance for all incoming packages. When this option is selected, we will immediately add layers of extra protection with your parcels.
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Scroll down the right part of the page and you will see “Order Extra Service in Advance” Within the option, select the “Extra Protection” then, click the Save button and we will add that protective layer as soon as we receive your incoming parcel.
  • Order the Extra Protection service after we receive your package:
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Click the “Manage Packages” button.
    • This will lead you to your packages we have received. Now click on “Extras” button for your preferred package.
    • This will open a popup window from where you can select Extra Protection for your parcel and click on the Order button.
    • {% trans "We'll notify you by email once we've completed your extra protection request so you can authorize your packages shipment." %}
