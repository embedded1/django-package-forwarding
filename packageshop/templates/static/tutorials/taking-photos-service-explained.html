{% trans "How to request photos of my parcel?" %}

{% trans "Introduction" %}

{% blocktrans %} Our most popular service! Keep reading to see why. On your request, we'll take photos of your goods so you can verify from afar that everything is in order and nothing is missing or damaged. As with any of our extra services, you'll receive additional discounts by instructing us to take the photos in advance of every incoming package. To view the photos we've taken, just click on the package number in your control panel. This service is not available for consolidated packages. There are 2 ways to request Package Photos with your parcels. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Walkthrough" %}

There are 2 ways to request photos of your parcel:

  • Order the Package Content Photos service in advance (20% off retail service price): This method allows you to order the service in advance for all incoming packages.
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Scroll down the right part of the page until you see “Order Extra Service in Advance”. Within the option, find the package content photos option and select the number of photos you would like us to take.
  • Order the Package Content Photos service after we receive your package:
    • Log in to your Control Panel.
    • Click the “Manage Packages” button.
    • This will lead you to your packages we have received. Now click on “Extras” button for your preferred package.
    • This will open a popup window from where you will find the Package Content Photo option, select the number of photos for your parcel and click on the Order button.
    • {% trans "We'll notify you by email once we've taken the photos so you can view them and authorize your package shipment." %}
