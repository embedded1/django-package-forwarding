{% trans "Introduction" %}

{% blocktrans %} Customs. Yeah, we all dread them, but every international shipment must have complete and accurate customs declaration paperwork, it doesn't matter if the goods are new, used, or why you're buying them. You, as the "Importer" are responsible for an accurate declaration of the nature of your goods and their values. Your country's customs authorities might assess fines or take legal action if your customs declaration misleading or false. USendHome takes no responsibility for any misstatements of duty, whether by you or the merchant, so, let's get it right! Don't worry; it's not as hard as it seems! {% endblocktrans %}
