{% trans "Introduction" %}

{% blocktrans %} We offer affordable insurance for all of our shipping methods. Our insurance policy is very comprehensive and covers you against lost packages and/or damaged goods. If your package is lost, simply provide us with your USendHome order number, which you'll find in your control panel under the order history tab, along with the order invoice you received from the merchant. We need snapshots if your claim is for damaged goods, and make sure you keep all damaged goods and their original packing, because you might need you to send them back to us. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Please note that you must keep all damaged goods and their original packing as you may be instructed to return the goods back to us. We will get back to you once we finish processing your claim. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans "Upload the order invoice you received from the merchant" %}
